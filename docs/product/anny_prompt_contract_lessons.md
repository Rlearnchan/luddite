# Anny Prompt Contract Lessons

Status date: 2026-05-18

This note summarizes what the two manual anny dry runs taught us before a
production anny agent exists.

## AI Knowledge Institution Case

Topic: `AI 즉답 시대의 지식기관 역할`

Profile:

- Evidence-enriched dry run.
- Low political and investment risk.
- Strong source coverage after evidence fill.
- `needs_source` dropped to zero, but education/AI-effect claims still require
  cautious wording and manual fact-checking.

Useful pattern:

- Start from the convenience of instant AI answers.
- Move to the risk that the thinking process becomes compressed or skipped.
- Use Royal Observatory as one institutional example, not as the official view
  of all knowledge institutions.
- Add counterpoint: AI can also increase learning access.
- Use Korea bridge and institution examples late in the story as supporting
  connection, not as the main proof.

Lesson:

- A source-rich outline can still be non-broadcast-ready when it deals with
  education effects or cognitive claims.
- `source attached` and `fact checked` must remain separate states.

## Productive Finance Policy Case

Topic: `생산적 금융과 정책자금 전환`

Profile:

- Structure and guardrails pass.
- Evidence-thin, single-article basis.
- `needs_source` and `needs_fact_check` correctly remain high.
- `policy_effect_claim` and `investment_risk_claim` handling is validated.

Useful pattern:

- Frame the issue as a question about whether finance can share long-term risk,
  not as a recommendation to invest.
- Keep the tension between collateral/short-term return finance and AI/semiconductor
  long-cycle capital needs.
- Include National Growth Fund / policy finance as a debate, not a guaranteed
  solution.
- Add risk/counterpoint around government-directed finance, loss transfer, and
  inefficient investment.

Lesson:

- Finance/policy storylines should not move to enriched rewrite until official
  material, policy mechanism, independent investment-scale context, market view,
  and counterpoint evidence are filled.
- Avoid investment advice, price/return predictions, policy promotion, and
  corporate-promo framing.

## Common Contract Lessons

- Use phrase-level key beat aliases, not single-word matching.
- Keep rhetorical bridge slides distinct from factual claims.
- `production_checklist` slides are valid for dry-run hygiene, but later should
  move to notes, appendix, or internal production checklist.
- `source_refs` must be slide-specific because the same source can play different
  roles on different slides.
- Counterpoint or risk discussion is required for sensitive topics.
- `ready_for_prompt_design=true` does not imply production readiness.
